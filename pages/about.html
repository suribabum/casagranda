About Us

Prapurna Impex Ltd. has ventured into retailing of modular kitchens and sanitary ware in April, 2007, under the showroom name 'Casa Granda'. Since then it has become the leading retailer of home interiors in Hyderabad.

Prapurna Impex is the authorized India distributor for Scavolini modular kitchens, by far the leading Italian kitchen brand for last 25 years.

Casa Granda is also the Hyderabad's flag ship store for Kohler sanitary ware, the world's largest sanitary ware company from USA.

At Casa Granda quality and service takes precedence over everything else, so housing the best of the brands it helps the customer in providing best solutions for their dream home.
